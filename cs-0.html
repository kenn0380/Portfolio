Our Challenge

Address the website's current lack of consistency, while increasing revenue, and providing a more efficient process for the clients to maintain their inventory.

Primary and Secondary Personas

Based on our client interview, we returned to the ideation phase, and synthesised a secondary user persona. This individual is not concerned with how a product is made, or where it's from. The first thing she wants to know is the brand of the product.

Our primary persona still evaluates a product (and store's) value based on quality and uniqueness.

What worked for users:

  • The content contained in the main navigation was what participants said they would expect to be in each of the menu itmes.

What we changed:

  • In A/B testing, users preferred a CTA over a hyperlink for "Get Involved." Even though they commented that they think the "Donate" CTA should be there instead, we left it as "Get Involved," in alignment with the clients goals to see more volunteers and consistent members.

  • On the confirmation page for the events, we decided to bring more details above the fold. A testing participant noted that they wouldn't be sure what next steps would be, if the instructions weren't immediately displayed.
